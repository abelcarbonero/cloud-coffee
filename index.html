Dulces

A selection of expertly handcrafted pastries made with real butter and premium ingredients baked fresh every day. Favourites include almond croissants, seasonal danish and ham & cheese croissants.

Sandwiches

Available daily.

Inspired by the Fraser Valley's produce and meats, sandwiches on house made bread rotate through the seasons. Wholesome soups and salads are periodically featured as part of a select menu — check out our social media for availability.

Nuestro Café

We decided not to stop at just croissants and danish. Delectable menu items include lemon sugar buns, cookies, bars, cinnamon buns and sticky buns to name a few. Don't miss our Saturday line up featuring a wide array of glazed and filled doughnuts.

Date un capricho

Our custom roasted beans are thoughtfully crafted by Republica Coffee Roasters in Fort Langley, so whether it's an Americano, a cappuccino or a latte paired with one of our house made syrups, we've got you covered. Seasonal flavours, teas and juices are also available.

Sobre Nosotros

Coffee.Eat.Love.

En CloudCoffee creemos que las cosas más simples de la vida son casi siempre las más bellas y las que merecen ser compartidas. Hemos creado un espacio donde unir el diseño, el mejor café y a las personas. Un lugar en el que compartir nuestro amor por el mejor café, la decoración hecha a mano y sabores que te harán feliz. Pero por encima de todo hemos soñado con crear un espacio lleno de amor donde te sientas como en casa. Bienvenido a CloudCoffee.
